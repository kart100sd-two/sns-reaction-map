# 部活動の地域移行の是非 — 漫画生成プロンプト

生成枚数: キャラシート2枚 + 本番ページ3枚 = 計5枚

---

## Step 1: キャラクターシート生成（Character Lock用）

### キャラシート① 片桐 蓮（かたぎり れん）

- 役割: 中学校の若手教員・バスケットボール部顧問（31歳）
- 立場: 地域移行に賛成
- 恐れ: 部活指導で教員が燃え尽き、子どもに向き合えなくなる
- 望み: 地域と役割を分け、教員も生徒も無理なく続けられる活動環境

```
Character design reference sheet, white background, multiple expressions (tired smile, worried, determined, apologetic, relieved), full body and close-up face views. Japanese male junior high school teacher and basketball club advisor, age 31, short black hair, slightly tired eyes, navy tracksuit, whistle around his neck, carrying a tote bag full of papers. Slim build, earnest and gentle but visibly overworked. Anime-inspired semi-realistic manga style, clean line art, school color palette.
```

### キャラシート② 野口 真紀（のぐち まき）

- 役割: 中学生の保護者（43歳）
- 立場: 地域移行に反対・費用負担懸念
- 恐れ: 月謝や送迎負担で子どもが好きな部活を諦める
- 望み: 家庭の収入や住む場所に関係なく、子どもの活動機会を守る

```
Character design reference sheet, white background, multiple expressions (calm, anxious, frustrated, protective, thoughtful), full body and close-up face views. Japanese mother of a junior high school student, age 43, shoulder-length chestnut brown hair, light gray cardigan over a white blouse, holding a smartphone showing a household budget app. Warm but protective expression, composed posture with strong concern for her child. Anime-inspired semi-realistic manga style, clean line art, soft realistic color palette.
```
